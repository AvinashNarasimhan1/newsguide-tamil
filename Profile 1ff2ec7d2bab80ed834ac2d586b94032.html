Work Experience:

Having over 30 years of experience in the field of journalism and more than ten years of blogging about technology in Tamil and over 5 years of experience in teaching Digital Journalism.

Started career in Police cheithy investigative weekly, worked in Swathesamithran Daily, Buisness magazine, Muthaleedu, in Maalaisudar daily ( ASST– News Editor), and India Today Tamil,Associate copy editor.
Now Working as an Independent Journalist, technology columnist, Blogger and guest lecturer.

Written and writes for leading Dailies, Magazines, Online media Like, Vikatan.com, Tamil Hindu, puthiya Thalaimurai, Tamil computer, Your Story, Dinamani.com, Kumudam and so on.

Written more than 15 article series on topics like, Start-ups, Founders, new emerging technologies, Search engines, History of Web, Internet pioneers, AI and digital technologies.

Recently wrote series on Women start up founders, Computer women Pioneers and Social media.

Article on Tamil Hindu hailed the contribution to Technology writing by calling cybersimman blog as , ‘Wikipedia for the netizens’.

Also writes books on internet, technology and New media and also writes and edits a Newsletter on websites– apps ( https://cybersimman.substack.com/
